What impact do UV and light have on your home?
UV radiation is produced by the sun and is the primary cause of skin cancer. In addition to being an naturally occuring carcinogen it also speeds up the aging of your skin, weakens your eyes, and will cause sunburns. Within your home UV will also cause discoloration of decoration and excessive exposure can cause plastic materials to become brittle.
Visible light, measured in lux, is a general measure of light intensity. This number in and of itself is not cause for alarm, but should you find yourself feeling low during prolonged periods of low light you may find it beneficial to be assessed for Seasonal Affective Disorder which may be treated by introducing artificial light into your environment.
