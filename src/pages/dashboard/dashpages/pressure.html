What is ideal indoor barometric pressure?
There is actually not a widely recognized 'comfortable' pressure for homes; what is more important to watch for is trends. Sudden 'low' pressure relative to what is normal for the individual can cause headaches for some as the sinus tissue adjusts to the difference. Similiarly, those with arthritis will sometimes notice 'pain in their bones' as a storm front moves in, bringing with it a sudden change in barometric pressure.
